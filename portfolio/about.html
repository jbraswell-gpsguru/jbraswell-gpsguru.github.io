Biography



I am a UC Berkeley graduate with a degree in geography and a strong interest in Cartography and Geographic Information Systems (GIS). I'lve always had this profound interest with maps and cities. Ever since I was a little boy, I've collected maps. I also could recall my family getting our first family car equipped with a GPS system almost two decades ago. In my spare time, I like to travel and pay attention to Bay Area Sports.

I currently do GIS work for a disclosure company in the San Francisco Bay Area and have taken night classes in GIS, coding, and currently in the process of getting my masters in GIS/Cartography.
